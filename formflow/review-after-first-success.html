Short version

Leave a review only if one real approval test worked end to end: Preflight passed, the reviewer email arrived, the approve/reject link worked, and the linked Google Sheet showed the final status. If any one of those failed, use support first.

Review only after this passes

  1. The linked response spreadsheet is selected correctly.
  2. Preflight passes without blocking warnings.
  3. One internal reviewer receives the approval email.
  4. The reviewer can approve or reject from the decision link.
  5. The linked Google Sheet shows the expected status.

What helps in a review

Workflow

What kind of Google Forms approval you tested, such as leave request, access request, event application, or another small internal request.

Setup

Whether selecting the response sheet, adding the approver, and running Preflight were clear.

Email

Whether the reviewer email and approve/reject decision path worked as expected.

Result

Whether the linked Sheet status made the workflow easy to audit.
