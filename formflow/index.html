Frequently asked questions

Can Forms handle approvals natively?

Not as a full workflow. FormFlow adds reviewer routing, email steps, and status tracking.

Where is the status stored?

In the linked Google Sheet so operators can audit progress.

Is it for complex enterprise workflows?

It is best for lightweight operational approvals built around Google Forms.
