Boundaries

FormFlow is for lightweight Google Forms approval workflows. It is not a full procurement system, ticketing platform, CRM, or enterprise compliance suite.

  • Privacy and permissionsPlain-English context for the form, file, sidebar, and email permissions used by the approval workflow.
  • SupportSend the form context, failing step, and screenshot when Preflight or email delivery does not match.
  • Review after successAsk for an honest Marketplace review only after the first approval test works.

Frequently asked questions

Can Google Forms approve submissions natively?

Google Forms can collect requests, but it does not provide a full approve/reject workflow with reviewer emails and linked-sheet approval status by itself.

Where does FormFlow store approval status?

FormFlow writes status to the linked Google Sheet so operators can review the workflow record next to the form responses.

What should I test first?

Use one copied form, select the linked response sheet, add one internal approver, run Preflight, and submit one test response.
