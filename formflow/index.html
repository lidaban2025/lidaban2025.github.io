Failure causes

Where first approval tests usually break.

If a user installs but does not succeed, the failure usually happens in one of four places. This is the support path before asking for a review.

Sheet

Wrong response spreadsheet selected

Duplicated forms often create duplicated response Sheets. Select the Sheet linked to the exact form being tested.

Email

Reviewer email not delivered

Check Preflight, approver address, spam folder, and Workspace mail restrictions before adding more reviewers.

Account

Reviewer account mismatch

The person clicking approve or reject should use the intended reviewer Google account, especially in multi-account browsers.

Status

Decision did not write back

Re-check the linked Sheet selection and send the decision page plus Sheet status screenshot to support.

Boundaries

FormFlow is for lightweight Google Forms approval workflows. It is not a full procurement system, expense platform, ticketing platform, CRM, legal approval platform, or enterprise compliance suite. Keep the first workflow small enough to audit in one linked Sheet.

  • Privacy and permissionsPlain-English context for the form, file, sidebar, and email permissions used by the approval workflow.
  • SupportSend the form context, failing step, and screenshot when Preflight or email delivery does not match.
  • Review after successUse the checklist only after Preflight, reviewer email, decision link, and linked Sheet status all work.
  • Leave an honest Marketplace reviewIf those four checks worked for your form, a short review helps the next Google Forms operator trust the setup.

Frequently asked questions

Can Google Forms approve submissions natively?

Google Forms can collect requests, but it does not provide a full approve/reject workflow with reviewer emails and linked-sheet approval status by itself.

Where does FormFlow store approval status?

FormFlow writes status to the linked Google Sheet so operators can review the workflow record next to the form responses.

What should I test first?

Use one copied form, select the linked response sheet, add one internal approver, run Preflight, and submit one test response.

What workflow should I try first?

Try a small internal workflow such as leave request, access request, event application, or resource request before adding multiple reviewers or complex routing.

What evidence matters before a review?

Preflight passed, reviewer email arrived, decision link worked, and the linked Sheet status updated. If any of those fail, use support first.
