Quick start

Setup in 3 steps

  1. Open your Google Form and launch FormGuard from Extensions.
  2. Choose one limit type: total responses, choice quota, or close condition.
  3. Run a test submission and check the public form before sharing it.

FAQ

Common questions

Does it replace Google Forms?

No. It adds guardrails on top of your existing Google Form.

What should I test first?

Use a copy of the form, submit once, and confirm the close/limit behavior.

Where do I get help?

Use the FormSuite support link below and include the form use case plus what limit failed.
