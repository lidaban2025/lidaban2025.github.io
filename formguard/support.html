FormGuard — All-in-One Form Manager for Google Forms™

Getting Started

  1. Install FormGuard from the Google Workspace Marketplace
  2. Open any Google Forms™ document
  3. Go to Add-ons > FormGuard to open the sidebar
  4. Configure your desired features (limits, notifications, schedule)

FAQ

How do I set a response limit?

Open the FormGuard sidebar, enable "Response Limiter", enter your maximum number of responses, and optionally set a custom closed message.

Can I schedule a form to open and close automatically?

Yes! Use the "Scheduled Open/Close" feature to set specific dates and times for your form to automatically open or close.

What happens when a choice is eliminated?

When a choice reaches its capacity, it is automatically hidden from future respondents. Existing responses are not affected.

Does FormGuard store my data externally?

No. All settings are stored using Google's built-in Properties Service. No data leaves Google's infrastructure.
