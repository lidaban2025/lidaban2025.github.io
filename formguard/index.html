Frequently asked questions

Does Google Forms already have response limits?

Yes. Use native response limits for simple total caps. FormGuard is for per-choice quotas, close messages, alerts, and launch testing.

Can it limit each time slot separately?

Yes. Choice quota workflows can stop individual sessions, roles, or appointment slots after they fill up.

Should I test on a live form first?

No. Use a copied form with tiny quotas, then apply the same setup to the real form after it passes.
