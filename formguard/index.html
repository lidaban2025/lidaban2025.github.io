Frequently asked questions

Does Google Forms already have response limits?

Yes. Use native Google Forms limits for simple cases. FormGuard is for extra guardrails like choice quotas, close messages, alerts, and launch testing.

Should I test on a live form first?

No. Use a copied form with a tiny cap, then apply the same setup to the real form after it passes.

Can it limit choices inside a form?

Yes. Choice quota workflows can stop sessions, roles, or options after they fill up.
