Frequently asked questions

Does Google Forms already have response limits?

Yes. Google Forms supports native close dates and response limits for simple form-level stopping. Use those first when they solve the job.

Should I test on a live form first?

No. Start with a copy or disposable form, then apply the same rule to the real form after it behaves correctly.

Can it limit choices inside a form?

Yes. Use choice quota workflows when sessions, roles, or options should stop accepting more selections.
