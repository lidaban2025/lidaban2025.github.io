FAQ

Can Google Forms limit RSVP seats?

Not as a full reservation system. You can monitor manually or use FormGuard to close the form at a response cap.

Can I add a waitlist after the limit is reached?

Yes. Link one waitlist form or contact path from the closed-form message.

Does FormGuard guarantee exact seat inventory?

No. Use buffers and manual confirmation when exact seats matter.
