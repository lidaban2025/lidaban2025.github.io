FAQ

Can Google Forms hide choices natively?

Not as a native per-choice capacity feature. You need Apps Script or an add-on.

Is hiding better than closing the whole form?

For events with multiple sessions, hiding only the full choice keeps the other sessions open.

What should late users see?

Use a waitlist option, closed-choice message, or alternate contact link.
