Recommended setup

Keep the workflow simple enough to test before the form is shared.

1

Identify the capacity choice

Use one dropdown, multiple-choice, or checkbox item to represent the session, role, or slot that can fill up.

2

Track submissions against that choice

Do not only count total form responses if each option has its own capacity.

3

Remove or block the full option

When a choice reaches quota, update the public form and show a waitlist or alternate contact path.

FAQ

Why is a full option still selectable?

Google Forms does not automatically remove individual choices when they reach capacity.

Can I close only one option?

Use an add-on or Apps Script choice-quota workflow to stop the full option while keeping other options open.

Should I use a waitlist?

Yes, prepare a waitlist link or full-message before sharing the registration form.
