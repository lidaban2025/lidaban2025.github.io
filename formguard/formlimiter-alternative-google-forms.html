Short answer

FormLimiter is useful when your main job is closing a form after a deadline or response count. FormGuard is built for teams who need to control what happens after people start submitting: response caps, choice quotas, alerts, and launch rules.

This page is intentionally precise: FormGuard is not claiming to copy every FormLimiter feature. It is an alternative when your real problem is Google Forms limits and operational control.
