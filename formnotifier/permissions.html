Permissions

Why FormNotifier asks for Google permissions

FormNotifier runs inside Google Forms and sends notification emails only for the form workflows you configure.

Install FormNotifierOpen setup guide

Permission mapping

ScopePlain-English use
forms.addonsLets FormNotifier run as a Google Forms add-on.
forms.currentonlyLets it read the current form's questions and submitted response data for owner alerts, respondent confirmations, templates, and preflight checks.
script.send_mailLets it send owner notifications, respondent confirmations, and safe test emails when configured.
userinfo.emailLets it identify the installing account for owner notification defaults and support context.
script.scriptappLets it create and manage the form submit trigger that runs after responses arrive.
script.container.uiLets it display the sidebar inside the Google Forms editor.

What FormNotifier does not do

  • It is not a CRM, newsletter, cold email, or marketing automation platform.
  • It does not sell your form data.
  • It sends only the notification paths you configure and test.

Uninstall and support

You can remove FormNotifier from Google Workspace Marketplace's Manage Apps page. Before uninstalling, turn off notifications in the sidebar if a form is live.
