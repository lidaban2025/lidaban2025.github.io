Quick start

Setup in 3 steps

  1. Open the Google Form and launch FormNotifier.
  2. Choose owner alerts, respondent confirmations, or both.
  3. Send one test response and confirm the email content before launch.

FAQ

Common questions

Why was no email sent?

Check the respondent email field, template variables, and Gmail sending limits.

Can I email the form owner?

Yes. Use owner alerts for new submissions.

Can I customize the message?

Yes. Use the template fields and test with one response first.
