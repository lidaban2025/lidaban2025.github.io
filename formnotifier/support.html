Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up FormNotifier?

  1. Open your Google Form in edit mode.
  2. Click the puzzle piece icon (Add-ons) in the top toolbar.
  3. Search for "FormNotifier" and click Install.
  4. Grant the required permissions when prompted.
  5. Go to Add-ons → FormNotifier → Configure to set up your notification preferences.
  6. Add recipient email addresses and customize your email template.
  7. Save your settings. Notifications will be sent automatically on new submissions.

How do I use template variables?

You can include form response data in your email templates using double curly braces. For example, if your form has a question titled "Name", use {{Name}} in your template to insert the respondent's answer. Variable names must match your form question titles exactly.

Why am I not receiving notification emails?

If notifications are not arriving, check the following:

  • Verify that the recipient email addresses are correct in your configuration.
  • Check your spam/junk folder for the notification emails.
  • Ensure you have not exceeded your monthly notification limit (50 for the Free tier).
  • Confirm that the form trigger is active by going to Add-ons → FormNotifier → Configure.
  • Google Apps Script has daily email sending limits. If you send a high volume of emails across multiple add-ons, you may have reached this limit.

How do I send confirmation emails to respondents?

In the FormNotifier configuration panel, enable the "Respondent Confirmation" option. Your form must collect email addresses (via the built-in "Collect email addresses" setting in Google Forms) for this feature to work. You can customize the confirmation email template separately from admin notifications.

How do I check my notification usage?

Open Add-ons → FormNotifier → Configure. Your current usage and remaining quota for the month are displayed at the top of the configuration panel. Usage resets on the first day of each calendar month.

Can I use FormNotifier on multiple forms?

Yes. The Free tier supports up to 3 forms. The Pro tier supports unlimited forms. Each form has its own independent configuration and notification settings.

How do I uninstall FormNotifier?

Go to Add-ons → Manage add-ons, find FormNotifier, click the three-dot menu, and select "Remove". This will remove all stored settings and disable notifications for that form.
