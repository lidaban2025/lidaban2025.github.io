Frequently asked questions

Can it email the person who submitted the form?

Yes. Use a respondent confirmation when your form collects an email address.

Can it notify multiple people?

Yes. Use the team notification setup when more than one operator needs the alert.

What should I test first?

Send one owner-only test email, then add respondent confirmation after the first test works.
