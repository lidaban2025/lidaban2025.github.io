Send Google Forms confirmation email to the respondent and owner

Use this workflow when a form submitter needs a receipt and the form owner or team also needs an operational alert.

Quick answer

Native Forms alerts are not enough for both audiences.

Google Forms can collect email addresses and send basic receipts in some setups, but it does not give every team a clear respondent template, owner alert, merge fields, preflight check, and test-send workflow.

Manual path

Check built-in settings first.

  1. Open Settings in Google Forms.
  2. Collect respondent email addresses if your workflow allows it.
  3. Enable response receipts if the native option fits.
  4. Use the Responses tab email notification for owner awareness.
FormNotifier path

Use separate templates and test sends.

  1. Install FormNotifier from Marketplace.
  2. Create a respondent confirmation email.
  3. Create an owner or team alert email.
  4. Run Preflight and send one test before enabling live notifications.
Best fit

Registrations, bookings, intake forms, and support requests.

This guide fits workflows where the submitter needs proof of submission and the owner needs a formatted operational notification.
