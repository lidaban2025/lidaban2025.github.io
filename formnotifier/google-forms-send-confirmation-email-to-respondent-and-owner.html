Send Google Forms confirmation email to the respondent and owner

Use this when the submitter needs a receipt and the owner or team needs a separate operational alert after each Google Forms submission.

Quick answer

Respondent

Send a clear receipt.

Use a confirmation template when the submitter needs proof or next steps.

Owner

Send a separate alert.

Route the operational notification to the owner, team inbox, or internal reviewer.

Test

Send one test first.

Run Preflight and confirm delivery before enabling live notifications.

Boundary

Not marketing automation.

Use it for form-triggered operational emails, not newsletters or cold outreach.

Safe setup

  1. Copy the form or use a test submission.
  2. Create the respondent confirmation template.
  3. Create the owner or team alert template.
  4. Run Preflight and inspect the test email result.
