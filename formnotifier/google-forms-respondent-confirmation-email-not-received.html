Start with these checks

A missing respondent confirmation usually falls into one of two buckets: the add-on did not send a confirmation, or it sent one to a different address than the submitter expected.

  1. Confirm the Google Form response was actually submitted and appears in the response sheet or Forms responses tab.
  2. Confirm respondent confirmation is enabled, not only an owner alert or team notification.
  3. Check how the form collects the respondent email: Google account collection or a required email question.
  4. Confirm the confirmation template uses that exact respondent email source.
  5. Submit one test response through the public form link using your own inbox.
  6. Open the recent send report and compare the expected respondent address with the actual recipient shown there.
  7. Check spam, promotions, updates, quarantine, and domain-level mail filters for the recipient mailbox.
Do not diagnose mailbox delivery until you know which address the confirmation was sent to. The fastest fix is often correcting the respondent email source.

Common causes after submit

The form does not collect respondent email

If the form does not collect email automatically, the confirmation needs a required email question that stores a valid address.

The wrong email question is selected

A form can contain several email-like fields. Make sure the confirmation uses the one submitters actually fill in.

Only owner alerts are enabled

Owner alerts notify the operator. They do not automatically prove the respondent confirmation path is active.

The address is misspelled or invalid

One typo in the submitted email value can make the respondent think nothing was sent.

The email arrived but was filtered

School, company, and shared-domain mailboxes may move automated confirmation emails into spam or quarantine.

The live submit path was never tested

A saved template is not the same as a real public-form submission. Test the actual link users will open.

FAQ

Why did the owner get an email but the respondent did not?

Owner alerts and respondent confirmations are separate paths. Check that respondent confirmation is enabled and that it has a valid respondent email source.

Does Google Forms automatically send a custom confirmation email?

Google Forms can show an on-screen confirmation message and collect responses, but custom respondent email workflows usually need an add-on or script.

Should I use collected email or an email question?

Use the source that your form actually relies on. If you ask for an email in a normal question, make that question required and select it as the respondent email source.

What should I do before launch?

Run preflight, send a test email, submit once through the public form link, and confirm the recent send report shows the expected respondent address.
