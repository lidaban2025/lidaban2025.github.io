What happens after contact

Keep expectations crisp. Buyers should know what happens next before they email you.

Starter kit buyers

You reply with the current package details, confirm tier, and send the delivery archive plus the install guide after payment confirmation.

Setup package buyers

You confirm whether the workflow fits the fixed scope, then move into one small implementation rather than a vague discovery process.

Pilot buyers

You narrow the workflow to one real approval queue, then decide whether it is a product pilot or a setup-service engagement.
