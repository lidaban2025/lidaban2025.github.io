Privacy Policy

FormGuard — All-in-One Form Manager for Google Forms™  |  Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. What is FormGuard?

FormGuard is a Google Forms™ Editor Add-on that helps form owners manage response limits, choice elimination, email notifications, and scheduled form closures — all from a single tool.

2. Data We Access

FormGuard accesses only the data necessary to provide its features:

  • Google Forms data — form title, response count, form settings, and form items (questions/choices) within the form you install the add-on on.
  • Your email address — used solely to send you notification emails (e.g., when a response limit is reached).

FormGuard operates under the forms.currentonly scope, meaning it can only access the specific form where you have installed and authorized the add-on. It cannot access your other Google Forms or any other Google services.

3. Data Storage

All configuration and settings are stored exclusively using Google's built-in Properties Service (document-bound and user-bound properties). This means:

  • Your data stays within Google's infrastructure.
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on any external servers.
  • No databases, analytics platforms, or third-party services receive your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data or form data with any third parties. Period.

5. Data Retention

FormGuard stores settings only while the add-on is active on your form. If you uninstall the add-on, the document-bound properties associated with that form are no longer used. You may also clear settings from within the add-on interface at any time.

6. Security

Since all data is stored within Google's own services, it is protected by Google's security infrastructure. FormGuard does not introduce any additional data transmission or storage vectors.

7. Children's Privacy

FormGuard does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The add-on is intended for use by Google Forms owners and administrators.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the add-on after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
