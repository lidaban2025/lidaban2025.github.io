FormGuard

All-in-One Form Manager for Google Forms™

About

FormGuard is a Google Forms™ Editor Add-on that combines four essential form management features into one tool: response limiting, choice elimination, email notifications, and scheduled form open/close.

Features

  • Response Limiter — Automatically close forms after a set number of responses
  • Choice Eliminator — Remove answer choices after they reach capacity
  • Email Notifications — Get notified when forms receive responses
  • Scheduled Open/Close — Set forms to open or close at specific times
