Dependent dropdown or synced choices?

These are different jobs. Google Forms is limited for live respondent-time filtering. FormRanger fits owner-controlled choice updates from Sheets.

Quick answer

Use FormRanger when Sheets is the source list and the form owner controls updates. Use another conditional-logic pattern when respondents need live filtering.

FAQ

Can Google Forms make a live dependent dropdown from Sheets?

Not natively. Google Forms does not provide respondent-time filtering from a Sheets range.

What can FormRanger help with?

It can update supported Google Forms choice questions from selected Sheets data before respondents use the form.

What should I test first?

Use one copied form, one question, and two sheet values such as Alpha and Beta.
