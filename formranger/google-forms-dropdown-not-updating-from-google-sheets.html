Troubleshooting checklist

Dropdown not updating?

Google Forms dropdowns do not stay linked to a Sheets range by default. Check the source, mapping, update action, and public preview.

Quick answer

If the dropdown is stale, test one copied form with Alpha/Beta in Sheets, run Preflight, click Update now, then inspect the respondent-facing preview.

Three common causes

Source changed

The tab, header, column, blanks, or duplicate values changed after setup.

Question changed

The dropdown was deleted, duplicated, renamed, or changed to another type.

Preview was not refreshed

The editor can mislead you. Open the public form preview after updating.

Use the right tool

Rare change

Manual copy/paste may be enough.

Code-owned workflow

Apps Script works if you maintain IDs, triggers, and authorization.

Sidebar workflow

FormRanger gives a mapped update flow with Preflight and Update now.

Boundary Do not use a visible dropdown as proof of live inventory, paid booking, or reserved seats.

FAQ

Does Google Forms automatically sync dropdowns from Sheets?

No. A Forms dropdown stores choices inside the form.

What should I check first?

Check the source column, mapping, access, Preflight result, Update now action, and public preview.

Should I fix the live form first?

No. Start with a copied form and two obvious sheet values.
