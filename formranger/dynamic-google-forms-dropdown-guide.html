FAQ

Can Google Forms choices come from Google Sheets?

Yes. FormRanger maps selected sheet data to supported Google Forms choice questions.

What should I test first?

Test one copied form, one question, and two sample sheet values before using a real workflow.

Can it reserve options while someone fills the form?

No. It updates form choices from Sheets; it is not a live reservation or inventory-locking system.
