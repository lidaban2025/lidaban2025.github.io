FAQ

Can Google Forms auto refresh choices from Sheets?

Not natively. You need Apps Script or an add-on workflow to push values into the form.

Is this live sync?

Treat it as controlled refresh, then verify the public form after each update.

Can I update multiple questions?

Use separate source ranges and mappings for each question.
