FAQ

Why does my dropdown not update?

Google Forms does not read Sheet cells live; the choices must be pushed into the form question.

Do formulas work as a source?

Yes, if the final source range outputs clean values and the update runs after formulas recalculate.

Should I test in the editor or public form?

Always test the public respondent link before sharing.
