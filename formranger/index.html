Frequently asked questions

Does it replace FormGuard?

No. FormRanger syncs choices from Sheets. FormGuard controls limits, quotas, alerts, and close behavior.

Can it sync dropdowns and checkboxes?

Yes. It is built for Google Forms choice questions that should be maintained from spreadsheet data, including dropdown, checkbox, multiple-choice, and grid choices.

Should the sheet be the source of truth?

Yes. Keep the list in Sheets, then refresh the form choices from that source.

What should I test before launch?

Use a copied form first, refresh one question from a small Sheet range, then open the public respondent view to confirm the choices are visible.
