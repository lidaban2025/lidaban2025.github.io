What FormRanger Pro Does

FormRanger Pro is a Google Forms add-on that populates form question choices (dropdowns, multiple choice, checkboxes, grids) from Google Sheets data. It keeps your form choices in sync with your spreadsheet automatically.

Data We Access

FormRanger Pro requests the following permissions to function:

  • Form structure (forms.currentonly) — We read and update supported question choices in the current Google Form. We do not access other forms or form responses.
  • Spreadsheet data (spreadsheets) — We read spreadsheet metadata, sheet tabs, headers, and selected column values from spreadsheets you explicitly connect in the sidebar. We do not sell spreadsheet data or copy spreadsheet contents to external databases.
  • User email (userinfo.email) — We access your email address only to identify your account for licensing and support operations.
  • Container UI (script.container.ui) — Used to display the add-on sidebar and configuration dialogs within Google Forms.
  • Apps Script triggers (script.scriptapp) — Used to manage refresh/update automation for the configured mappings.

Data We Store

FormRanger Pro stores only the minimum configuration required to run the add-on inside your Google account. This can include the selected spreadsheet ID, sheet name, column name, question ID, and mapping preferences such as sorting or duplicate removal. We do not copy entire spreadsheet datasets into external storage.

Data Storage

All user settings (such as question-to-column mappings and update preferences) are stored exclusively in Google Apps Script's Properties Service. This data remains within Google's infrastructure and is associated with your Google account and the add-on project.

We do not operate an external database for form or spreadsheet content. Spreadsheet values are read from Google Sheets at runtime and used only to build the updated choice list inside the current Google Form.

Data Protection

FormRanger Pro relies on Google's security controls for data in transit and at rest within Google Workspace and Apps Script. We do not independently export spreadsheet contents, store Google credentials, or maintain separate copies of linked spreadsheet data on our own servers.

  • Access is limited to spreadsheets the user explicitly connects for a mapping.
  • Spreadsheet data is processed only for the choice-sync function requested by the user.
  • Only lightweight mapping configuration is persisted; raw spreadsheet rows are not retained outside Google services.
  • Support requests are handled through email; users should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive sheet data in support messages.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties. Your data remains within the Google ecosystem at all times.

Data Retention

Settings are retained in Google Properties Service for as long as the add-on is installed. Uninstalling the add-on removes all stored settings. We do not retain any data after uninstallation.

Security

FormRanger Pro runs entirely within Google's Apps Script platform, inheriting Google's authentication, transport security, and infrastructure protections. We do not maintain separate passwords, tokens, or customer-hosted databases for the spreadsheet content used by the add-on.
