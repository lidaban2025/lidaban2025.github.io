FAQ

Can I avoid Apps Script?

Yes, an add-on can handle the choice update workflow without you maintaining scripts.

When is Apps Script better?

Use Apps Script if you need custom logic and you are comfortable owning triggers and errors.

Does this replace Google Sheets?

No. Sheets remains the source of truth; the add-on updates Google Forms from it.
