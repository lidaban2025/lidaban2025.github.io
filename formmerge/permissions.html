Permissions

Why FormMerge asks for Google permissions

FormMerge runs inside Google Sheets and sends personalized operational emails from the rows and templates you configure.

Install FormMergeOpen setup guide

Permission mapping

ScopePlain-English use
spreadsheets.currentonlyLets FormMerge work with the spreadsheet you open and authorize.
drive.fileLets it work with files created or opened through the add-on flow where needed.
script.send_mailLets it send test and batch emails from the configured rows.
userinfo.emailLets it identify the installing account for sender context, test sends, and support context.
script.scriptappLets it manage triggers used for configured email merge workflows.
script.container.uiLets it display the sidebar inside Google Sheets.

What FormMerge does not do

  • It is not CRM, newsletter, cold email, or marketing automation software.
  • It does not sell sheet data.
  • You should use it only for permission-based operational email and respect Gmail quotas and anti-spam rules.

Uninstall and support

You can remove FormMerge from Google Workspace Marketplace's Manage Apps page. Disable any active email workflow before uninstalling when possible.
