Frequently asked questions

Is FormMerge for newsletters?

No. It is positioned for operational row-based email, not marketing automation or newsletter campaigns.

Can I use HTML email?

Yes. The setup supports HTML templates and merge tags from sheet columns.

What should I test first?

Send one row to yourself, verify the merged values, then send a small batch.

What if recipients do not receive the message?

Check the recipient column, spam or quarantine folders, the send report, and Gmail sending limits before changing the workflow.
