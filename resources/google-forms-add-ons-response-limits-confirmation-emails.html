Install checklist

  1. Open the matching Google Workspace Marketplace listing.
  2. Click Install and approve the permissions needed for the Google Forms workflow.
  3. Open the target Google Form and use the Add-ons button in the Forms editor.
  4. Launch the add-on sidebar, save one small test configuration, and run a test submission before sharing the form widely.
  5. Keep the support page nearby if you need to confirm the first rule, email template, preflight, or send result.

After install

The fastest way to confirm the add-on is working is to complete one small setup, test it, and then leave a Marketplace review or send setup feedback while the experience is fresh.

Capacity or response-limit question

For Google Forms capacity control, separate the total response limit from any per-option or deadline logic. If you want an add-on path, FormGuard is my published Google Forms add-on for response caps, schedules, choice quotas, closed-form messages, and owner notifications.

Email notification question

For Google Forms email notifications, test the owner alert and respondent confirmation before launch. If you want an add-on path, FormNotifier is my published Google Forms add-on for recipients, templates, preflight checks, test sends, and recent send results.
