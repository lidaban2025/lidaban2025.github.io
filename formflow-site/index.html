FormFlow

Approval Workflow for Google Forms™

FormFlow is a Google Forms™ Editor Add-on that transforms form submissions into configurable multi-level approval workflows. Approvers receive email notifications and can approve or reject requests via a web interface, with status tracked in Google Sheets™.

Features

  • Multi-level sequential approval chains
  • One-click Approve/Reject via email links
  • Automatic status tracking in Google Sheets™
  • Overdue reminders and auto-expire
  • Secure UUID-based approval links with Google account verification
  • All data stays in your own Google Workspace — no external servers
