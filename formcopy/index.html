Frequently asked questions

Why do formulas break in response sheets?

Google Forms appends new rows, and helper formulas often do not automatically fill the way teams expect.

Can it copy formatting too?

Yes. The workflow is designed for formulas, helper columns, and formatting.

Should I use a template row?

Yes. Keep one known-good row as the source for future response rows.
