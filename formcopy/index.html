Frequently asked questions

Does FormCopy replace my formulas?

No. It uses your model row as the source and copies the prepared logic into target rows.

Do I need coding?

No. The workflow is configured from the Google Sheets sidebar.

What should I test first?

Use row 2 as the model, submit one sample response, and confirm the new row receives the expected formulas and formats.
