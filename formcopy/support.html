FAQ

Common questions

Why do formulas disappear?

Google Forms appends new rows, so helper columns often need explicit copy-down behavior.

Should I test on live data?

No. Test with a fake response or a copied sheet first.

What should I report?

Share which columns should copy and what happened on the latest submitted row.
