## FormGuard listing

### Recommended Marketplace title
FormGuard: Choice Limits, Quotas & Timers for Google Forms

### Short description
Add response limits, option quotas, email notifications, and timers to Google Forms for registrations, workshops, classes, and appointments.

### Category keywords
- Google Forms choice limit
- Google Forms option quota
- limit Google Forms responses by option
- Google Forms capacity limit
- Google Forms appointment slots
- Google Forms workshop registration
- Google Forms timer
- Google Forms email notifications

### First-screen value proposition
Google Forms is great for simple forms, but it does not give you enough control when seats, time slots, or choices have limited capacity. FormGuard adds the missing controls: total response limits, per-choice quotas, notifications, and timers.

### Long description draft
FormGuard helps organizers, teachers, HR teams, and small businesses run Google Forms with real capacity controls.

Use it when you need to:
- close a form after a total response limit is reached
- limit how many people can choose each option
- manage workshop seats, class sections, appointment slots, or event time slots
- receive email notifications for new responses or limit events
- add a timer for quizzes, assessments, or timed forms

Common examples:
- 20 seats per workshop session
- 5 people per appointment slot
- limited class section capacity
- event registration with sold-out choices
- internal signup forms where each option has a maximum count

FormGuard is designed for people who want to keep using Google Forms instead of switching to a heavier form builder.

Docs: https://formsuite.dev/formguard/google-forms-choice-limit.html

## FormRanger listing

### Recommended Marketplace title
FormRanger: Dynamic Choices from Google Sheets for Google Forms

### Short description
Sync Google Sheets data into Google Forms dropdowns, multiple choice, checkboxes, and grid choices without maintaining Apps Script.

### Category keywords
- Google Forms dropdown from Google Sheets
- dynamic Google Forms choices
- populate Google Forms from spreadsheet
- Google Forms multiple choice from Sheets
- Google Forms checkbox choices from Sheets
- Google Forms grid choices from Sheets
- auto update Google Forms options

### First-screen value proposition
Stop manually editing long dropdowns in Google Forms. FormRanger keeps form choices synced from Google Sheets so lists stay easier to maintain.

### Long description draft
FormRanger helps you keep Google Forms choices updated from a Google Sheet.

Use it when you need to:
- populate a dropdown from Google Sheets
- update multiple choice, checkbox, or grid options from a sheet
- maintain long option lists in one spreadsheet
- avoid copy-pasting choices into Google Forms
- avoid maintaining your own Apps Script automation

Common examples:
- class rosters
- event/session lists
- product or inventory lists
- department/team lists
- location or appointment options
- any form where choices change often

FormRanger is for teams that want the flexibility of Google Sheets and the simplicity of Google Forms.

Docs: https://formsuite.dev/formranger/dynamic-google-forms-dropdown-guide.html
