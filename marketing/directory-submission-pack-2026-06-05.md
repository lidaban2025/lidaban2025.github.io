## Founder note draft

I built FormSuite because I kept seeing Google Forms users solve the same problems with fragile scripts or manual editing.

FormGuard is for capacity problems: limited seats, sessions, appointment slots, and forms that should close automatically.

FormRanger is for choice-maintenance problems: dropdowns or multiple-choice lists that should come from Google Sheets instead of being copy-pasted into Forms.

The goal is not to replace Google Forms. It is to keep people inside the Google Forms workflow when they only need a few missing controls.
