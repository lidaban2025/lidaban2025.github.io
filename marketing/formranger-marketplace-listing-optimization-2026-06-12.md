## Recommended long description

FormRanger helps Google Forms owners update choice questions from Google Sheets without maintaining Apps Script.

Use it when your source list lives in a spreadsheet and your form needs current dropdown, multiple-choice, checkbox, or grid choices.

Typical workflows:

- keep class or training session choices in Google Sheets
- update event, location, department, or product options
- refresh checkbox lists from a maintained spreadsheet column
- test a copied form before sharing a live form

How it works:

1. Open FormRanger inside a Google Form.
2. Select the source spreadsheet, sheet tab, and column.
3. Map the source list to a supported Google Forms choice question.
4. Run Preflight to check the setup.
5. Click Update now.
6. Open the public respondent preview and confirm the choices changed.

Supported question types:

- dropdown
- multiple choice
- checkbox
- multiple-choice grid choices
- checkbox grid choices

FormRanger is not a booking, payment, or inventory-lock system. It updates the form owner’s choice list from Sheets before respondents use the form.

Free during public launch.
