## Core product positioning

### FormGuard

One-line positioning:
FormGuard helps Google Forms users control capacity per choice: workshop seats, class sessions, appointment slots, event options, waitlist/final message, and deadline/response controls.

Primary link:
https://formsuite.dev/formguard/google-forms-choice-limit.html

Marketplace/add-on page:
https://formsuite.dev/formguard/

Avoid leading with generic “response limit” because Google Forms/basic add-ons already cover parts of that. Lead with **per-choice quota / session capacity / option-level seats**.

### FormRanger

One-line positioning:
FormRanger syncs Google Forms dropdowns, checkboxes, multiple-choice, and grid choices from Google Sheets without maintaining Apps Script.

Primary link:
https://formsuite.dev/formranger/dynamic-google-forms-dropdown-guide.html

Marketplace/add-on page:
https://formsuite.dev/formranger/

Lead with **Sheets → Forms choices sync**, stale dropdown prevention, public-preview test, and no Apps Script maintenance.
