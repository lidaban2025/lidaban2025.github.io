Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up FormMerge Pro?

  1. Open the Google Sheets spreadsheet linked to your Google Form responses.
  2. Click Extensions → Add-ons → Get add-ons.
  3. Search for "FormMerge Pro" and click Install.
  4. Grant the required permissions when prompted.
  5. Go to Extensions → FormMerge Pro → Configure.
  6. Select the email column (the column containing recipient email addresses).
  7. Write your email subject and body using {{Column Header}} merge tags.
  8. Save your settings. Emails will be sent automatically on new submissions.

How do merge tags work?

Merge tags use double curly braces with your column headers. For example, if your spreadsheet has columns "Name" and "Score", you can write: Hi {{Name}}, your score is {{Score}}. Each email will be personalized with the values from that row.

How does FormMerge Pro detect the email column?

FormMerge Pro auto-detects columns containing email addresses by scanning column headers and values. You can also manually select the email column in the configuration panel if auto-detection does not pick the right one.

Can I send a test email before going live?

Yes. In the configuration panel, click "Send Test Email". A test email will be sent to your own email address using the first data row, so you can preview exactly what recipients will receive.

What if some rows were not emailed?

Use the Batch Send All Unsent feature. Go to Extensions → FormMerge Pro → Batch Send. This sends emails for all rows that do not yet have a "Sent" status in the status tracking column.

How do I track which emails have been sent?

FormMerge Pro automatically adds a status column to your sheet. Each row shows "Sent" with a timestamp when the email was successfully sent, or "Error" if sending failed. This makes it easy to see at a glance which responses have been processed.

Why are my emails not being delivered?

If emails are not arriving, check the following:

  • Verify that the recipient email addresses in your spreadsheet are correct.
  • Ask recipients to check their spam/junk folder.
  • Ensure you have not exceeded your monthly email limit (50 for the Free tier).
  • Google Apps Script has daily email sending limits. If you send a high volume across multiple add-ons, you may have reached this limit.

How do I uninstall FormMerge Pro?

Go to Extensions → Add-ons → Manage add-ons, find FormMerge Pro, click the three-dot menu, and select "Remove". This will remove all stored settings and disable automatic email sending.
