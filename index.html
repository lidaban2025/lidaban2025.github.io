Google Workspace add-ons

One toolkit for document merges, approval flows, formula automation, form controls, and notifications.

FormSuite is a collection of focused Google Workspace add-ons for teams that run on Forms, Sheets, Docs, and Gmail. Each product solves one operational problem well, and the products are designed to work together without adding extra servers or external dashboards.

Getting started

A simple path if you are evaluating, onboarding, or reviewing one of the add-ons.

1. Open the product page

Start from the product overview to confirm the add-on fits your workflow and see the main features at a glance.

2. Use the support guide

Each support page includes setup steps, common questions, and the exact Google Workspace context where the add-on runs.

3. Return to the add-on

Once the setup steps are clear, go back to Google Forms or Google Sheets and complete the flow inside the add-on sidebar.
