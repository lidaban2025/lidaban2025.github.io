Something not working?

Do not reinstall random add-ons. First confirm the product, run one small test, then send the exact result.

Contact supportFirst-run checklist

Send this, and support is faster.

1. Product and Google app

Example: FormNotifier in Google Forms, or DocForge in Google Sheets.

2. Expected vs actual

Say what you expected, what happened, and where it failed.

3. One test result

Use a copied form, sample row, or your own inbox. Add a screenshot if possible.

Contact support

Email: support@formsuite.dev

Include the add-on name, the Google app you used, what you expected, what happened, and one screenshot if possible.
